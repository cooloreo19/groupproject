Parietal Lobe

The PARIETAL LOBE is where the majority of sensory signals and nerve communication
occurs. Which helps determine warmth, cold, pain, touch, and
even determine our surroundings! In fact, this is where the left hemisphere
is more involved with the right side and the right hemisphere is
more involved with the left side of our bodies.
